One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest

Overview

One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest (1975) is a psychological drama directed by Miloš Forman and based on Ken Kesey's novel. It follows Randle McMurphy, a rebellious criminal who fakes insanity to avoid prison and is sent to a mental institution, where he challenges the oppressive Nurse Ratched.

Cast & Creative Team
  • Jack Nicholson as Randle McMurphy
  • Louise Fletcher as Nurse Ratched
  • William Redfield as Harding
  • Brad Dourif as Billy Bibbit
  • Directed by: Miloš Forman
Main Characters

Randle McMurphy: A rebellious man who challenges the hospital’s strict rules.

Nurse Ratched: A cold and authoritarian head nurse who controls the ward.

Chief Bromden: A silent patient who plays a crucial role in the story.

Reviews
